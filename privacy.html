Privacy Policy

Last updated June 12, 2026

Clear Vision Eye Center ("we," "us," or "our"), the practice of Dennis A. Chuck, M.D., respects your privacy. This policy explains what information this website collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies only to this website. It is not a substitute for the Notice of Privacy Practices you receive as a patient of the practice under HIPAA.

The short version

  • We only collect the information you choose to send us through the appointment request form.
  • We use it for one thing: to contact you and help schedule your care.
  • The form is delivered by a third-party service (FormSubmit). It is convenient, but it is not a secure medical channel, so please don't send diagnoses, test results, or medical history through it.
  • We don't sell your information. Ever.

Information we collect

Information you give us

When you submit the appointment request form, we collect the details you enter: your name and title, phone number, email address, your reason for visiting, your preferred contact method, and any message you include. These fields are optional except your name and your consent to be contacted.

Information collected automatically

This is a simple static website. We do not set our own tracking cookies. Some embedded content, such as the Google Maps frame on the contact section, is provided by Google and may collect usage information under Google's own policies. The optional language preference you choose is stored only in your own browser (local storage) so the site remembers it on your next visit; it never leaves your device.

How we use your information

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your request, schedule or confirm appointments, and answer your questions. We may keep a record of your message as part of providing care. We do not use your contact details for marketing without your permission.

How the appointment form works

The appointment request form is processed by FormSubmit, a third-party form-delivery service. When you submit the form, your entries are sent through FormSubmit's servers and emailed to our front desk. This is handled by FormSubmit under their privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

Because this path is not an encrypted, HIPAA-compliant channel, please do not include sensitive medical information in the form. Share only what we need to reach you and understand the general reason for your visit. For anything detailed or sensitive, please call us at (909) 622-1188.

Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We share it only with the service providers that help us run this website (such as FormSubmit for form delivery and our email provider), and only as needed to respond to you, or when required by law.

Data retention

We keep appointment requests for as long as needed to respond and to maintain reasonable business records, then dispose of them. You may ask us to delete a request you sent by contacting us.

Your choices

  • You can skip the form entirely and simply call or email us instead.
  • You can ask us what request information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it.
  • You can clear the saved language preference at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for adults arranging eye care for themselves or their family. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children through this site.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices or the law change. We will revise the "Last updated" date above when we do.
