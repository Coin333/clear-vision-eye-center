Accessibility Statement

Last updated June 12, 2026

Caring for sight is our life's work, so making this website usable for people with disabilities, including those who themselves have low vision, matters deeply to us. Clear Vision Eye Center is committed to ensuring that pomonaeyedoctor.com is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology.

Our standard

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological conditions.

What we have built in

  • Semantic, structured HTML with clear heading levels and landmark regions.
  • A "Skip to content" link for keyboard and screen-reader users.
  • Visible keyboard focus states on links, buttons, and form fields.
  • Text alternatives (alt text) for meaningful images, and decorative graphics hidden from assistive technology.
  • Color combinations chosen to meet AA contrast ratios for text and interface elements.
  • Form fields with associated labels and clear, spoken error messages.
  • Respect for the "reduce motion" setting, which turns off non-essential animation.
  • Responsive layouts that work with zoom and on small screens without loss of content.
  • Content available in English, Spanish, and Chinese.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is a continual process, not a one-time task. We review the site as we make changes and work to fix issues as we find them. Some third-party content embedded here, such as the Google Maps frame, is outside our direct control, and we encourage those providers to maintain their own accessibility.

Need help, or found a barrier?

If you have trouble accessing any part of this website, or if you need information from this site in a different format, please tell us. We will do our best to provide what you need and to fix the problem. You can also complete any task on this site by simply calling our office, and a member of our team will gladly assist you.
